Digital Media in Review

Introduction

I am a sophomore studying multimedia journalism at Virginia Tech. I have learned a great deal about digital media this semester. I have learned the importance of having a firm standing in both the creative realm of liberal arts, and the technical realm of digital media. Throughout this course, the VT-shaped student has been emphasized. Being a multi-disciplinary student is extremely important, but so is being an involved student. This goal of this website is to give physical representations of what it means to be a VT-shaped student, and what elements are missing from this concept of the VT-shaped student.
