V-Shaped Video


video

The V-shaped video is the portrayal of the transformation of a student from being uninvolved in the early stages of college, to become well-rounded as a college student. The "v" in the VT-shaped student is focused on communal learning. In this video, we take a look inside the life a freshman English major, who is in search for an internship. He is optimistic about his chances of finding an internship at Business Horizon's, until he is faced with the fact that his resume is extremely empty. Through this experience, he learns the importance of applying one's skills to extra-curricular activities. This allows one to make connections for future career opportunities, and it looks good to hiring companies to see that their candidates are experienced not only in academics but also in community.
