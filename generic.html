My Interests

My name is Josh Gann, and I am a sophomore studying multimedia journalism and professional and technical writing at Virginia Tech. I have recently gotten involved with the sports branch of Collegiate Times. I am also involved in Cru, a campus ministry at Virginia Tech. I am a part of their Media Team, and will be able to design for them this semester. I have always enjoyed writing, and over the last year I have worked hard on improving my content, and adapted to the style of writing that pertains to media writing. However, I did not know much in relation to the technological side of writing.

The VT-shaped student was a foreign concept to me entering this semester. However, it became familiar throughout the first month of taking Writing in Digital Media. I now realize how important it is to have the ability to combine knowledge of writing and the English language, with the knowledge of publishing material and of the HTML language.

My interests have evolved since the beginning of the semester. I came into this semster wanting to work exclusively in writing. However, I am now fascinated by the coding and publishing process. I am also loving learning how to edit photos in photoshop in one of my other classes. I am gaining interest in the multimedia side of this subject, and am looking forward to acquiring more knowledge in both areas of multimedia journalism.
